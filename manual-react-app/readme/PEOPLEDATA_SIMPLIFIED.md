# PeopleData - Simple Design

## What’s Different

**Before:**  
- Fancy colors and effects  
- Lots of shadows and icons  
- Uppercase and emojis  
- Complicated styles

**After:**  
- Light gray background  
- Simple blue highlights  
- No icons or emojis in titles  
- Normal text  
- Clean, easy layout

---

## Design Approach

- Uses soft colors and simple fonts  
- Friendly, clear language  
- Easy to read and understand  
- Simple borders and rounded corners  
- Consistent look everywhere

---

## Main Features

- Simple header: "Famous People"  
- Easy search bar  
- Friendly counter: "Found X people"  
- Clean cards with round images  
- Clear sections: "Scientists", "Leaders & Activists"  
- Smooth, gentle interactions

---

## Language

- Uses natural, friendly phrases  
- Short, clear descriptions  
- Proper grammar

---

## Benefits

- Easy to read  
- Professional look  
- Accessible  
- Simple to use  
- Friendly and calm  
- Works well on mobile

## Summary

The new design is clean, simple, friendly, and professional—great for everyone!
